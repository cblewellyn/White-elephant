Rules/ How to Play:
1. Everyone who wants to play will enter thier name into the contest.
a. you can enter in late and as long as "LAST PICK" isn't showing.
b. there must be the same amount of gifts as players.
c. you should try to enter everyone into the contest, prior to starting. Each name will appear in the Name Bank.
2. If your name is picked, you must pick out of the pile of gifts, or steal a gift that is already been opened.
a. A gift can only be stolen 3 times (max), after which it is locked.
b. the first person, will also go last.
3. Must be willing to have fun!
